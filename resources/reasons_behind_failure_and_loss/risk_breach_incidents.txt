================================================================================
                       RISK BREACH INCIDENTS LOG
================================================================================
                Risk Limit Violations and Exposures
================================================================================

PURPOSE:
--------
This log tracks all risk limit breaches, exposure violations, and risk
management system failures. Effective risk management is critical in HFT to
prevent catastrophic losses and ensure regulatory compliance.

SCOPE:
------
- Position limit violations
- Loss limit breaches
- Exposure concentration events
- Leverage limit violations
- VaR/CVaR breaches
- Volatility exposure violations
- Margin call situations
- Counterparty risk breaches
- Regulatory limit violations
- Risk system failures
