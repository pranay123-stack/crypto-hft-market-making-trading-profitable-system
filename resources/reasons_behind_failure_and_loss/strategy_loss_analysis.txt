================================================================================
                       STRATEGY LOSS ANALYSIS LOG
================================================================================
          Trading Strategy Performance Failures and Loss Attribution
================================================================================

PURPOSE:
--------
This log is dedicated to analyzing trading strategy underperformance, algorithm
failures, unexpected losses, parameter misconfigurations, and market condition
mismatches. It provides a framework for understanding why a strategy lost money
and what can be done to prevent future losses.

SCOPE:
------
- Strategy algorithm failures
- Parameter misconfiguration
- Alpha decay and strategy degradation
- Market regime changes
- Model prediction errors
- Execution quality issues
- Risk management failures
- Position management problems
- Signal generation errors
- Market microstructure changes
