================================================================================
                       POST-MORTEM ANALYSIS TEMPLATE
================================================================================
              Comprehensive Incident Review and Learning Framework
================================================================================

PURPOSE:
--------
This template provides a structured framework for conducting thorough post-mortem
analyses after significant incidents. The goal is not to assign blame, but to
understand what happened, why it happened, and how to prevent similar incidents
in the future. A well-executed post-mortem transforms incidents into learning
opportunities and system improvements.

POST-MORTEM PRINCIPLES:
-----------------------
1. BLAMELESS: Focus on systems and processes, not individuals
2. FACTUAL: Base analysis on data and evidence, not speculation
3. COMPREHENSIVE: Examine all contributing factors
4. ACTIONABLE: Produce concrete improvement actions
5. TRANSPARENT: Share learnings broadly
6. TIMELY: Conduct while details are fresh

WHEN TO CONDUCT A POST-MORTEM:
-------------------------------
- P0/P1 severity incidents
- Incidents with significant financial impact (>$10K)
- Incidents with regulatory implications
- Recurring incidents (3+ occurrences)
- Incidents revealing systemic issues
- Near-miss events with high potential impact
- Incidents requested by management
