================================================================================
                        INSURANCE COSTS - HFT SYSTEM
================================================================================

OVERVIEW: Insurance costs represent 3-4% of total 3-year TCO ($300K-$450K).
Essential coverage for technology firms engaging in financial trading.

3-YEAR INSURANCE COSTS SUMMARY:

1. CYBER LIABILITY INSURANCE
   Purpose: Data breaches, cyberattacks, business interruption
   Coverage: $5M - $10M
   Annual Premium: $50,000 - $100,000
   3-Year: $150,000 - $300,000

2. ERRORS & OMISSIONS (E&O) INSURANCE
   Purpose: Professional liability, trading errors
   Coverage: $5M - $15M
   Annual Premium: $40,000 - $75,000
   3-Year: $120,000 - $225,000

3. DIRECTORS & OFFICERS (D&O) INSURANCE
   Purpose: Executive liability protection
   Coverage: $2M - $5M  
   Annual Premium: $15,000 - $30,000
   3-Year: $45,000 - $90,000

4. COMMERCIAL GENERAL LIABILITY (CGL)
   Purpose: Office liability, general business risks
   Coverage: $1M - $2M
   Annual Premium: $5,000 - $10,000
   3-Year: $15,000 - $30,000

5. PROPERTY INSURANCE
   Purpose: Office equipment, servers (if applicable)
   Annual Premium: $8,000 - $15,000
   3-Year: $24,000 - $45,000

6. BUSINESS INTERRUPTION INSURANCE
   Purpose: Loss of income due to system failures
   Coverage: $5M - $10M
   Annual Premium: $20,000 - $40,000
   3-Year: $60,000 - $120,000

7. CRIME/FIDELITY INSURANCE
   Purpose: Employee theft, fraud
   Coverage: $1M - $3M
   Annual Premium: $10,000 - $20,000
   3-Year: $30,000 - $60,000

TOTAL INSURANCE (3 YEARS): $444,000 - $870,000
AVERAGE ANNUAL: $148,000 - $290,000

YEAR-BY-YEAR:
Year 1: $160,000 - $310,000 (higher initial premiums)
Year 2: $142,000 - $280,000 (some discounts)
Year 3: $142,000 - $280,000

KEY FACTORS AFFECTING PREMIUMS:
- Trading volume and asset classes
- Security posture and controls
- Prior claims history
- Coverage limits and deductibles
- Number of exchanges and jurisdictions

OPTIMIZATION STRATEGIES:
- Higher deductibles reduce premiums 15-25%
- Bundle policies with one insurer for 10-20% discount
- Strong security controls reduce cyber premiums 20-30%
- Claims-free history reduces Year 2-3 premiums
- Captive insurance for large operations

MINIMUM COVERAGE: $200,000 - $350,000 over 3 years
RECOMMENDED: $444,000 - $870,000 over 3 years

END OF DOCUMENT
