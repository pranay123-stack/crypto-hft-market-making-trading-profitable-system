8. SLA MEASUREMENT & REPORTING
================================================================================

MEASUREMENT METHODOLOGY
------------------------------------------------------------
- All measurements use high-resolution timestamps (nanosecond precision)
- Latency measured end-to-end from ingress to egress
- Outliers beyond 3 standard deviations are investigated
- 99.9th percentile and above are reported separately
- Measurements taken continuously in production

REPORTING FREQUENCY
------------------------------------------------------------
Metric Type          | Internal | Management | Stakeholders
---------------------|----------|------------|-------------
Real-time Dashboard  | 1 second | 1 minute   | N/A
Operational Reports  | 15 min   | Daily      | Weekly
SLA Compliance       | Daily    | Weekly     | Monthly
Trend Analysis       | Weekly   | Monthly    | Quarterly

SLA VIOLATION RESPONSE
------------------------------------------------------------

Severity  | Response Time | Escalation           | Action
----------|---------------|----------------------|------------------
Warning   | < 15 minutes  | Team Lead            | Investigate
Critical  | < 5 minutes   | Engineering Manager  | Immediate fix
Emergency | < 1 minute    | CTO + CEO            | System rollback
