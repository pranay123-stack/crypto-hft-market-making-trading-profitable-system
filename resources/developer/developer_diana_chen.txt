   - Assess exposure
   - Notify management immediately
   - Preserve evidence for investigation
   - Generate incident report

3. Risk Check Failure
   - Identify failed risk checks
   - Assess impact on trading
   - Investigate root cause
   - Implement fix or bypass (with approval)
   - Test thoroughly
   - Monitor closely after fix

4. Position Reconciliation Failure
   - Compare system vs broker positions
   - Identify discrepancies
   - Investigate missing/duplicate fills
   - Correct position data (with approval)
   - Document reconciliation
   - Prevent recurrence

5. P&L Discrepancy
   - Verify P&L calculation logic
   - Check market data prices
   - Review all trades
   - Recalculate if needed
   - Identify source of discrepancy
   - Correct and document
