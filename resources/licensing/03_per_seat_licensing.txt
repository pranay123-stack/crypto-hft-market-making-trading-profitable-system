PER-SEAT LICENSING MODEL
Pricing Based on Number of Trading Desks
================================================================================

DEFINITION OF "SEAT":

A seat is defined as:
- One concurrent user/trader accessing the system
- One trading desk (physical or virtual)
- One set of credentials for platform access

Not counted as separate seats:
- Backup/failover systems (same trader)
- Development/test environments
- Read-only access for risk managers

PRICING TIERS:

STARTER TIER: 1 Seat
- Price: $10,000/month ($120,000/year)
- Includes: 5 exchange connectors
- Volume cap: $10M monthly trading volume
- Support: Business hours
- Use case: Small prop firm, single trader

PROFESSIONAL TIER: 3 Seats (expandable)
- Base price: $35,000/month ($420,000/year)
- Additional seats: $8,000/month each
- Includes: 10 exchange connectors
- Volume cap: $100M monthly
- Support: 24/7 priority
- Use case: Medium trading desk

ENTERPRISE TIER: Unlimited Seats
- Price: $100,000-$500,000/month (custom)
- Includes: All exchanges, unlimited volume
- Support: Dedicated 24/7, <15min response
- Use case: Large market maker, institution

SEAT MONITORING:

Technical Implementation:
- Authentication system tracks concurrent sessions
- License server validates seat usage
- Real-time monitoring dashboard
- Automated alerts for overages

Overage Handling:
- Soft limit: Warning, 7-day grace period
- Hard limit: Additional seats billed automatically
- Overage fee: 1.5x normal seat price (incentive to upgrade)

COMPLIANCE AND AUDITING:

Annual Audit Rights:
- Licensor may audit seat usage annually
- 30-day advance notice required
- Licensee must provide usage logs
- Underreporting penalty: 3x underpaid amount

Self-Reporting:
- Monthly usage reports required (Professional and Enterprise)
- Automated reporting through system
- Good faith disputes resolved collaboratively

SEAT TYPES:

Production Seats (billable):
- Live trading
- Real market data
- Production environment

Development Seats (50% discount):
- Testing and development
- Historical data only
- Non-production environment
- Limited to 20% of production seats

Read-Only Seats (25% discount):
- View-only access
- For risk managers, compliance
- No trading capability
- Unlimited for Enterprise tier

TRUE-UP PROCESS:

Quarterly True-Up:
- Compare actual vs licensed seats
- Bill for overage or credit for underutilization (if annual contract)
- Adjust going-forward license

Example:
Licensed for 5 seats at $8K each = $40K/month
Actual usage: 7 seats average
Overage: 2 seats × $8K × 3 months = $48K quarterly true-up
Option: Upgrade license to 7 seats going forward

BENEFITS OF PER-SEAT MODEL:

For Licensor:
- Predictable recurring revenue
- Easy to understand and explain
- Scales with customer usage
- Aligns pricing with value delivered
- Simple to track and enforce

For Licensee:
- Transparent pricing
- Pay only for what you need
- Easy to budget
- Can start small and scale
- Flexibility to adjust seat count
