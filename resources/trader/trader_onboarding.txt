1.2 PROGRAM OVERVIEW
-------------------

PROGRAM STRUCTURE:

The onboarding program consists of four phases:

PHASE 1: ORIENTATION (Week 1)
- Introduction to firm and trading desk
- Setup and access provisioning
- Culture and values
- Meet the team
- Initial assessments

PHASE 2: CORE TRAINING (Weeks 2-4)
- Trading platform mastery
- Risk management fundamentals
- Market structure knowledge
- Strategy frameworks
- Compliance and regulations
- 8-10 hours/day of training

PHASE 3: STRATEGY TRAINING (Weeks 5-8)
- Deep dive into trading strategies
- Strategy-specific training modules
- Backtesting and analysis
- Performance metrics
- 6-8 hours/day of training + self-study

PHASE 4: SIMULATED TRADING (Weeks 9-12)
- Paper trading with real market data
- Strategy implementation
- Performance monitoring
- Feedback and coaching
- Full trading day simulation

PHASE 5: SUPERVISED LIVE TRADING (Weeks 13-16)
- Small capital allocation (10-25% of target)
- Real money trading
- Close supervision
- Gradual expansion
- Performance evaluation

Total Duration: 16 weeks
Expected Commitment: 50-60 hours/week
Success Rate: 70-80% complete program successfully

LEARNING APPROACH:

Our training uses multiple modalities:
- Classroom instruction
- Self-paced online learning
- Hands-on practice
- Simulation trading
- Supervised live trading
- Mentorship
- Peer learning

You'll be assessed continuously throughout the program to ensure
you're mastering each component before advancing.
