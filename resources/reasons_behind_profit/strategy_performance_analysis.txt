================================================================================
                         EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
================================================================================

This document provides a systematic framework for analyzing WHY specific
trading strategies are profitable. The goal is to understand the fundamental
drivers of strategy performance, validate assumptions, and identify
opportunities for improvement.

KEY QUESTIONS THIS DOCUMENT ANSWERS:
1. What is the source of alpha in each strategy?
2. How consistent is strategy performance across market conditions?
3. Which strategy parameters have the most impact on profitability?
4. Are strategies performing as expected vs backtests?
5. How can strategies be improved or scaled?

ANALYSIS FRAMEWORK:
- Signal Quality Assessment
- Prediction Accuracy Measurement
- Parameter Sensitivity Analysis
- Market Condition Dependency
- Interaction Effects
- Degradation and Capacity Analysis
