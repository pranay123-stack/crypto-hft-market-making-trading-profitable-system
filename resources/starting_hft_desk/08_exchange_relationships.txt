================================================================================
EXCHANGE RELATIONSHIPS
================================================================================

Priority Exchanges: Tier 1: Binance, Coinbase, Kraken (start here). Tier 2: 
OKX, Bybit, Huobi, KuCoin (add next). Tier 3: Regional and specialized (later). 
Onboarding Process: Corporate account application (2-4 weeks), KYC/AML 
documentation (company formation docs, beneficial owners), Trading permissions 
and limits, API key generation (keep secure!), Whitelisting IP addresses, Test 
trading (small size). Institutional Accounts: Higher API rate limits, Better 
fee tiers (0.02-0.08% vs 0.1-0.2% retail), Dedicated account manager, Direct 
support channel, Credit lines (for derivatives). Negotiation: Volume 
commitments for lower fees, Colocation or direct connectivity, Early access to 
new products, Market maker programs (rebates for liquidity provision). Typical 
Fees: Maker: 0.02-0.08% (you provide liquidity). Taker: 0.04-0.12% (you take 
liquidity). Market maker rebates: Up to -0.01% (get paid to make markets). Key 
Contacts: Maintain relationships with exchange bizdev, Attend exchange events 
and conferences, Join market maker groups.
