================================================================================
REGULATORY COMPLIANCE
================================================================================

US Requirements: Trading own capital: No registration needed (proprietary 
trading). Managing others' money: Register as Commodity Pool Operator (CPO) or 
Investment Adviser. CFTC registration: If trading futures/swaps for customers. 
FinCEN: Register as MSB if crypto-to-fiat. State licenses: Money transmitter 
licenses (if applicable). AML/KYC: Written AML program, Customer due diligence, 
Suspicious activity reporting (SAR), Transaction monitoring. International: EU: 
MiCA compliance (if EU clients). Singapore: MAS licensing. UK: FCA 
authorization. Compliance Program: Designate CCO (Chief Compliance Officer), 
Written policies and procedures, Annual training, Regular audits. Cost: 
Legal/regulatory counsel: $100K-$300K/year. Compliance staff: $150K-$250K. 
Software/tools: $50K-$100K/year.
