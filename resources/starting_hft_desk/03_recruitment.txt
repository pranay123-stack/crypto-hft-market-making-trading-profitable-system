================================================================================
RECRUITMENT STRATEGY
================================================================================

Where to Find Talent: LinkedIn (target HFT firms), Referrals (best quality), 
University recruiting (MIT, Stanford, CMU), Conferences (QuantCon, Trading 
Show). Compensation: Quant Developers: $250K-$350K total comp (70% cash, 30% 
bonus). Traders: $200K base + 15-25% profit share. Risk: $180K total comp. 
Equity: 10-20% pool for team, vesting 4 years. Signing bonuses: $25K-$100K for 
key hires. Interview Process: Technical screen, system design, trading case, 
culture fit. Look for: C++ expertise, trading knowledge, startup mentality, 
execution focus.
