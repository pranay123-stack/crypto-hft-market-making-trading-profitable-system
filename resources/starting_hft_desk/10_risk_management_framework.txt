================================================================================
RISK MANAGEMENT FRAMEWORK
================================================================================

Risk Policies: Written risk policy document, Board-approved risk limits, Daily 
risk reporting, Monthly risk committee meetings. Position Limits: Per-symbol 
position limit ($500K-$2M depending on liquidity), Total gross exposure ($20M 
with $10M capital = 2x leverage), Net exposure limit (e.g., max $5M net long or 
short), Concentration limits (max 30% in any single asset). Loss Limits: Daily 
loss limit ($100K-$250K), Trader-level limits ($50K-$100K per trader/day), 
Strategy-level limits, Monthly/quarterly drawdown limits. Pre-Trade Controls: 
Order size limits, Price collar (orders must be within X% of mid), Duplicate 
order prevention, Sufficient balance checks. Real-Time Monitoring: P&L 
dashboard (update every second), Position monitor, Exposure calculations, 
Alerts for limit breaches. Post-Trade Analysis: Daily P&L attribution, Trade 
reconstruction, Exception reports, Performance analytics. Circuit Breakers: 
Auto-stop trading if daily loss limit hit, Manual kill switches (per trader, 
per strategy, global), Require manager override to resume. Risk Systems: 
In-house development or 3rd-party (Pnew Relic, Risk, etc.). Real-time 
calculation engine, Integration with OMS/EMS, Automated alerts and circuit 
breakers. Risk Team: Risk Manager (reports to CEO, not trading desk), 
Independent from traders, Veto power over risky trades, Monthly board risk 
report.
