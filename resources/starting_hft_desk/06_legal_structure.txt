================================================================================
LEGAL STRUCTURE
================================================================================

Entity Choice: LLC (pass-through taxation, simpler) vs C-Corp (better for VC, 
stock options). Recommendation: Start LLC, convert to C-Corp if raising 
institutional capital. Jurisdiction: Delaware (standard for finance/tech, 
established corporate law). Incorporation: File Certificate of Formation 
($200), Obtain EIN from IRS, Draft Operating Agreement (LLC) or Bylaws (Corp), 
Issue membership interests or stock. Operating Agreement: Capital 
contributions, Profit/loss allocation, Management structure, Voting rights, 
Transfer restrictions, Dissolution provisions. Cost: DIY: $500-$1,000. Law 
firm: $5K-$15K (recommended). Ongoing: Annual franchise tax (Delaware: $300 
LLC, $400+ Corp), Annual report, Registered agent ($100-$300/year).
